Steps to Glowing Skin
Step 1
Clean Slate
Spray Vitamin C Cleanser lightly on your face. Remove oils, dirt, and makeup with a cotton pad. Make sure to shake the bottle before applying so that its ingredients activate and combine.
Step 2
Nutrient Boost (Night)
Give your skin a precision measured dose of 2% stabilized Vitamin C. Using the provided plastic cap, break a Vitamin C Ampoule and pour the concentrate into your hand. Apply to the face, neck, and decolletage during the evening.
Step 3
Serum Soak (Day)
Tap the bottom of #3 Clear Skin Elixir in the palm of your hand to settle the bubbles. Press 1-2 pumps of the Elixir into your hand and massage across your face. Let dry for 10 minutes before bed and wear overnight.
Step 4
Barrier Seal
Seal in hydration with a lightweight face cream designed to maintain the skin's natural water reserve and stimulate cellular renewal. Gently apply the Vitamin C Face cream to your face and neck in an upward motion.

Avocado Oil

Rich in Vitamins A, D, & E

Carrot Root Extract

Carrot Root Extract is packed with nutrients, vitamins, and antioxidants. It helos to boost collagen and may minimize the appearance of redness.

Cold-Pressed Kiwi Oil

Made with over 60% Omega-3 Alpha-Linolenic Acid which is extremely nourishing. Kiwi Seed Oil is ideal for combating wrinkles, fine, lines, and skin blemishes.
